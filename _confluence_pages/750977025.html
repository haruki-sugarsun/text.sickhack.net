Overview

This page summarizes Haruki Sato / 佐藤 春旗's activities and experiences during my long vacation ( Past Announcements - Haruki Vacation 2024-04~08 ) from April 20th to September 8th, 2024.

Thanks to the sabbatical, I could focus on what I needed, wanted, and was genuinely interested in. I hope this report helps readers understand my experience and provides information for those interested in similar adventures.

While I didn’t achieve everything, I would rate the sabbatical 8 out of 10. During this time, I achieved several goals:

  • 📚 Learning: I learned several topics, including coaching and pre-MBA courses.

  • 🗺️ Travel: I visited several places to expand my knowledge and experiences (and for fun )

  • 🎭 Personal Projects: Besides visiting great places, I also spend time on things I’ve wanted to do/know/experience so far. Some are for fun, future goals, or experimentation.

  • 🏥 Medical Care: I explored the medical care I was interested in.

However, I also had some un-achievements, where I missed or failed in executions, particularly in health, fitness, and time management.

Sabbatical 2024 - Reports - Medical Care
During my sabbatical, I explored medical treatments for androgenetic alopecia (AGA) and medical hair removal. While not essential, both treatments have the potential to enhance the quality of life. The timing of the sabbatical provided a perfect opportunity to research and consider these options in more depth.

(See Reports - Medical Care for details)
