Overview

This page collects

My personal Mail service configurations

Using Thunderbird - https://www.thunderbird.net/ja/, I set multiple email accounts and enable

  • Favorite Folders

  • Unified Folders

in the side panel.

For the “Folder Subscription”, basically, I disable it for some uninteresting folders, including:

  • folders for tests

  • folders for long-term archives, which I don’t want to sync/copy in the local

    • FILES*, dumps-archives, …

and create some “Search Folder”

  • Inbox 01D

    • Choose “Inbox” from the accounts

    • Ages in days < 1

  • Inbox 05D

    • Choose “Inbox” from the accounts

    • Ages in days < 5

  • Inbox 10D

    • Choose “Inbox” from the accounts

    • Ages in days < 10

I then update the account settings for the accounts to disable server sync for

  • Archive folders

  • Spam folders

  • Trash folders
