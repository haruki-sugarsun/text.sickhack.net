Conflict with “Buttercup”

As of 2024/03/31, the Chrome extension for Office Online conflicts with the Confluence Cloud editor.

https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/heflipieckodmcppbnembejjmabajjjj

This conflict makes some components (iframe, short link card, for example) in the editor blink, making it hard to edit.
