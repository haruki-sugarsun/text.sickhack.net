Rice Cooking with パール金属 ご飯も炊けるマグカップ

  • https://amzn.asia/d/5oaguvc

  • Steps

    1. for 1 合 rice, add 190ml water.

    2. 浸水 10~30min

    3. 500W 4~5min, 200W 4~5min

    4. Stir

    5. Wait for 10min
